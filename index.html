Solving the Off-Campus Housing Crisis, One Verified Listing at a Time

A 14-member team at Delta State University, Agbor applied the five-stage Design Thinking process to tackle one of the most persistent challenges in student life: finding safe, affordable, and legitimate off-campus accommodation.

Our Solution

Prototype Detail

A Verified Listings Platform Built for Students

The prototype is a lightweight mobile web application designed to bridge the gap between students seeking off-campus accommodation and landlords offering genuine spaces near the university.

Landlords register and submit rooms with photos, pricing, proximity to campus, and availability status. Every listing undergoes a basic verification check before going live. Students can filter by budget, location, and room type — then initiate direct contact without middlemen or hidden fees.

Problems Uncovered

  • Fraudulent agents taking deposits for non-existent rooms
  • Long daily commutes causing student stress and fatigue
  • No central, trusted source of off-campus listings
  • Unsafe or unsuitable housing accepted out of desperation
  • Landlords with no platform to reach verified student tenants

Why Digital Won

  • Accessible on any smartphone — no app install needed
  • Scalable beyond a single notice board or social group
  • Enables verification workflows impossible on paper
  • Direct landlord contact removes exploitative middlemen
